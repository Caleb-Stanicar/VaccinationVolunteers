About Us

What We Do

VaccinationVolunteers is a service that both alerts people that they may be eligible for the COVID-19 vaccine as well as provides young volunteers to help people who have trouble using the internet.

Get Involved

If you would like to volunteer time to helping people through registering for a vaccine, click ‘sign up’ and you will receive training materials and a schedule to do so.

The Team

We are four undergraduate students at Columbia University. We noticed our grandparents were having trouble being notified they were eligible for a vaccine; we wanted to create a solution to solve this situation.
