COVID-19 Vaccinations New Jersey

Information:

Step 1:
https://covid19.nj.gov/pages/vaccine

 

 

Step 2:
Once on the page, have them navigate to the tab on whether they qualify. If they do, navigate to the vaccine registration option Once there, navigate to the second item on the list linked as “View locations here”. Help the caller find their area and explore the options for centers provided the vaccine If the location has a number, suggest they call that number to register If they want to register online, help them navigate the individual clinic’s registration.

 

 

Step 2(a):
If they do not qualify, point them towards the pre-registration option to be notified when they are eligible.
